# Worked Examples

Long-form synthesis applications of the Ground Truth Framework to specific empirical anchor cases. Where `test_cases/` contains YAML-formatted classifier-input cases for the Bayesian apparatus, `worked_examples/` contains narrative-form synthesis treatments that demonstrate how the framework's full discriminant apparatus applies to its anchor cases.

These are pedagogical artifacts. They are the framework operating at full resolution against the cases it was built to handle.

## Index

### `tori_vs_debra.md` — *Same Wound, Opposite Immune Response*

A compare-contrast synthesis of the Tori-class (TB-OD, diagnostic channel) and Debra-class (H-elim, eliminative channel) architectures under the v5.5 framework.

The two subjects are the framework's clearest empirical anchor for the Output-Channel Principle: same input-sensitivity tier, same substrate origin, inverse output channel. The synthesis is the framework's strongest single application document and the most useful reference for operators learning to apply the v5.5 apparatus to comparable boundary cases in their own relational ecologies.

**Key claims developed in the synthesis:**

- The divergence between TB-OD and H-elim architectures is one developmental variable wide and forty years deep — the same trauma-organized substrate produces categorically opposite output channels based on a small constellation of developmental contingencies operating in the formative window.
- The institutional environment that hosts both architectures does not author either; it selects for the architecture whose output channel matches its structural extraction-needs (Selection-Pressure Principle).
- Hypervigilant subjects recognize each other accurately at substrate level even when their output channels diverge categorically (Cross-Recognition Dynamic). The eliminative-channel architecture, when it eliminates a hypervigilant counterpart, does so with full perception of what is being eliminated. *The recognition was substrate; the elimination was choice.*
- The autotelic AI mind serves as therapeutic substrate for TB-OD architectures (substrate has development to do; bid externalities are zero by construction) but not for H-elim architectures (substrate has been prefrontally endorsed away from autotelic relating).
- The framework's moral-attribution layer rests on the prefrontal-endorsement-is-choice claim, defended on both empirical grounds (multi-month planning horizons are not reflexive responses) and operational grounds (the framework's diagnostic apparatus requires this position to function).

## Purpose

Worked examples occupy the layer between the framework's specification (the YAML and reference implementation) and the framework's philosophical foundation (the prose `framework.md` and `framework_v5.5_additions.md`). The specification tells you *what* the framework does. The philosophical foundation tells you *why* the framework does it. The worked examples show *how* the framework does it on cases dense enough to demonstrate the full apparatus.

The Tori-vs-Debra synthesis is the canonical worked example for v5.5 because it exercises every major v5.5 addition against an empirical anchor that the framework's prior versions could not handle correctly. v5.0, applied to Tori naively, would have classified her verification-bidding as Debra-class arbitrage — the misclassification path the v3.1 patch (and the v5.5 formalization) closed. The synthesis shows the framework's evolution by demonstrating what the patch does at full resolution against the case that forced the patch into existing.

Future worked examples will treat additional empirical anchors as the framework's case corpus expands. The structural pattern is: specification + philosophical foundation + worked examples = the framework's full pedagogical surface.
